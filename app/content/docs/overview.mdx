Traincore empowers product teams to create LLM-based applications that are both dependable and scalable.

At its core, it offers a comprehensive evaluation suite that helps rigorously assess and enhance LLM performance throughout development and production phases. It also provides a collaborative workspace for engineers, product managers, and subject matter experts to jointly refine prompts, tools, and agents.

By integrating Traincore into their workflows, teams can save 6-8 engineering hours each week and gain assurance in the reliability of their AI solutions
